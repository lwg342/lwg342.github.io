    • 1273 Habsburg family begins reigning over Europe: Rudolf I of Habsburg became king of Germany.

    18th Century

    1700 - 1780: French population grew from ~20 million in 1700 to ~30 million in 1780. (From Piketty)

    1766-1834: Thomas Malthus, English cleric, scholar and influential economist in the fields of political economy and demography.

    1772-1823: David Ricardo, British political economist, one of the most influential of the classical economists along with Thomas Malthus, Adam Smith and James Mill.

    1817: On the Principles of Political Economy and Taxation by David Ricardo was published. Established the principle of scarcity.

    1798: Thomas Malthus published Essay on the principle of population
