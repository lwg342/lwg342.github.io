    Chapte 1 State Pricing

    Reading Notes

    About state pricing: derivation from no arbitrage condition. Uses Separating Hyperplane Theorem. Also derived from agent maximization problem.

    Notice that when we derive it from agent’s maximization problem, it’s related to marginal utility which has more economic meaning than derived from arbitrage.

    The state price vector, the risk neutral probability and state-space deflator play central roles in asset pricing.
